About Us

Opioid addiction is a long-lasting (chronic) disease that can cause major health, social, and economic problems. Opioids are a class of drugs that act in the nervous system to produce feelings of pleasure and pain relief.

The purpose of this project was to provide data sets to the users for conducting research, developing web and mobile applications, design data visualizations.
This Web-based interactive tool provides users to choose various options for retrieving datasets related to Opioid prescription drug abuses

Prescription Vs Opioid Death Statistics

The dataset shows the geographic distribution in the United States of retail opioid prescriptions dispensed per 100 persons per year compared against the Opiod related deaths. Click here for the full dataset

Filter By State

You can filter the dataset for required states. For eg:, if you would like to filter the dataset by Texas, Arkansas, provide the query as state=Tx,Ar .
Click here to filter by state

Filter By Year

You can filter the dataset for required years. For eg:, if you would like to filter the dataset by 2010, 2011, provide the query as year=2010,2011 .
Click here to filter by year

Filter By State and Year

You can filter the dataset for required states and years. For eg:, if you would like to filter the dataset by Texas and Arkansas for the year 2010 and 2011, provide the query as state=Tx,Ar/year=2010,2011 .
if you want to get the data for Texas and Arkansas for all the years, query=Tx,Ar/year=all .
Click here to filter by State and year
